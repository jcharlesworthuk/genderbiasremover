Head of CRM & Tech

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Head of CRM & Technology with a specific focus on CRM systems, websites, and analytics to join our team. As the Head of CRM & Technology, you will be responsible for leading and managing our marketing technology stack, with a particular emphasis on CRM system implementation and optimization, website development and maintenance, and data analytics. Your role will involve collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive marketing strategies, enhance customer experiences, and maximize the effectiveness of our marketing initiatives.

Main Responsibilities
CRM Management:
Oversee the implementation, configuration, and ongoing management of the CRM system to support our marketing and sales processes.
Develop and optimize workflows, dashboards, and reports to enhance lead management, pipeline tracking, and customer relationship management.
Provide guidance and training to marketing and sales teams on CRM system functionalities and best practices.
Continuously evaluate and recommend improvements to the CRM system to streamline processes and improve efficiency.

Website Development and Maintenance:
Lead the development, enhancement, and maintenance of our company websites, ensuring an optimal user experience, functionality, and performance.
Collaborate with design, content, and development teams to create engaging and conversion-focused website experiences.
Manage website projects, including timelines, budgets, and resources, ensuring timely delivery of high-quality results.
Stay up to date with industry trends and emerging technologies to recommend website enhancements and implement best practices.

Data Analytics and Reporting:
Develop and implement a robust data analytics strategy, leveraging tools such as Google Analytics or similar platforms.
Define key performance indicators (KPIs) and build dashboards and reports to monitor marketing campaign effectiveness, website traffic, lead generation, and other relevant metrics.
Analyze data to uncover actionable insights, drive data-driven decision making, and optimize marketing strategies and tactics.
Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders and executive leadership.

Integration and Automation:
Identify opportunities for integration and automation within the marketing technology stack to improve efficiency and data synchronization.
Collaborate with IT and other relevant teams to integrate the CRM system with other marketing tools and platforms for seamless data flow and process automation.
Streamline marketing processes through automation, ensuring consistent and efficient execution of campaigns and lead management.

Vendor Management:
Evaluate, select, and manage relationships with vendors and service providers related to marketing technology, including CRM consultants, web development agencies, and analytics providers.
Negotiate contracts, manage budgets, and ensure vendors deliver high-quality services that meet our requirements and objectives.

Requirements
Bachelor's or master's degree in a relevant field.
Proven experience in marketing technology management, with a focus on CRM systems, websites, and analytics.
In-depth knowledge and hands-on experience with CRM systems, including implementation, configuration, and optimization.
Proficiency in website development and maintenance, including content management systems (CMS) and web analytics.
Strong analytical skills and experience working with data analytics platforms to derive actionable insights.
Familiarity with marketing automation platforms, email marketing tools, and other relevant marketing technologies.
Project management skills with the ability to lead and coordinate website development projects.
Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams and stakeholders.
Strategic mindset with the ability to align marketing technology initiatives with overall business goals.
Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to adapt to a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
Experience in vendor management and contract negotiation is preferred.
