HR Coordinator

Purpose of the HR Coordinator role:

You will be primarily responsible for ensuring that all recruitment and HR related duties are completed in a timely and accurate manner, and act as the first point of contact for employee enquiries on recruitment, training and policies, and assistance throughout the employment lifecycle.

Responsibilities:

- Provide day to day HR operations support including recruitment, on-boarding, off-boarding, and probations.
- Support contract managers and employees by advising on policies and procedures.
- Assist in and manage employee relations cases, where appropriate including absence management and performance management.
- Ensure that prospective staff have the right to work at the organization.
- Plan and if required to deliver training, including new staff inductions.
- Ensuring renewals of checks are applied for and completed in a timely manner.
- Provide support to employees in various HR-related topics such as leaves and compensation and resolve any queries that may arise.
- Submit and finalize the monthly payroll cycle.
- To undertake any other reasonable duties as requested by HR Manager.
- Serve as a primary user of HR systems & maintain expertise in systems, processes, and security to enable accurate submissions and reporting.
